Cheese Pizza

Description

The homemade pizza for those who are tired of those store bought frozen things, have a lot of time on their hands and hate toppings

This is a recipe for the first time pizza makers that is also very delicious, while being simle it can also be topped with any ingredients people might like, this also leaves room for anyone who might want to have their own personal pizza, no more need to compromize on pizza night

Ingredients

  • 1 prepared pizza dough
  • 1/3 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Position an oven rack in the lower third and preheat the oven to 475F.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a non-stick baking sheet.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge. Sprinkle with cheeses.
  4. Bake for 12 to 14 minutes, or until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbling and beginning to brown. Let cool for a few minutes before cutting.
